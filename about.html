Carolina Gómez-Montoya (B.A., Portland State University; M.A. and Ph.D. University of Maryland, College Park) is a professor of Spanish Language, Literature and Seminar at Bard High School Early College (BHSEC Newark). Previously, she taught at BHSEC Queens and at the University of Maryland, College Park. She is interested in studying the intersections between writing, thinking and travel. She has published articles and fiction in Literal: Latin American Voices/Voces Latinoamericanas; ¡Basta! Mujeres colombianas contra la violencia de género (Bogotá: Debate escrito, 2015) and is a contributor to Letra vuelo. She lives and writes in Brooklyn, NY.
